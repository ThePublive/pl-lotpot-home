Our Story

Mr. A.P BAJAJ, launched India's first kids magazine-LOTPOT in 1969. We are a well- established in New Delhi, India based publishing & content producing company reputed for high levels of quality & content. We provide our clients solutions for - Publications, Media, Entertainment, Advertising, Digital, Retail at national level.

Popular characters of Lotpot Comics "Motu Patlu" have a 3D animated show successfully running since the last 11 years on Nickelodeon India TV Channel and still continues entertaining kids with its rib tickling content. Apart from Motu Patlu, we have numerous other popular characters from Lotpot Comics ready to deliver in animation.
